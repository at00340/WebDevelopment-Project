Player Mechanics

Rogue Lineage is a roblox game created by the developers at Monad Studios. A rouge-like PVP centered game whos progression, combat, and story have been heavily influenced by the community.

Races

All the races of Gaia. Each has different characteristics and abilities.

Classes

The classes a player can obtain. Each with a different moveset.

Spells

Spells that the player can learn. some are locked to ones class.

Equipment

Equipment the player can use. some are locked to certain classes but each has their own stats

Artifacts

Rare and powerful items a player can obtain, giving them a new ability.
