Status

Status effects In Rogue can be inflicted onto a character with multiple ways. Environment, Player skills, and natural occurances are all ways a players will encounter status effects. Some of these effects can be beneficial, while others can make the game far more diffiult to play. These status effects will come in different forms and are often dealt with in seperate ways.

Injury Type

The injury type status effects are the most common. Injuries are status effects that can be cured if the player goes to see a doctor for a certain amount of Silver.

Status Name Effects
Frostbite Once a players tempature drops below a certain amount, they will get the frostbite energy. if some time passes and their tempature doesnt increase, they will rapidly lose health till becoming an ice statue
Burn marks Once a players tempature reaches a certain amount or a special move is used, they gain burn marks. player will take 25% more damage
Broken ribs When an enemy uses certain moves, the player will get a broken rib. The player will be ragdolled for longer when affected.
Broken leg When an enemy uses certain moves, the player will get a broken leg. once a players leg is broken they can no longer run or dodge forward and are slowed
Broken arm When an enemy uses certain moves or the player falls from a certain height they will get a broken arm and can neither climb nor carry other.
Slash wound When an enemy uses certain moves, the player will be afflicted with a slash wound. once hit, the player will have -25 hp until the Injury is fixed

Insanity Type

Rogue Lineage contains a mechanic called sanity. It is a status that can only be effected through certain ways. Insanity comes in the form of stages, often progressed by whatever the insanity source is. These statuses can only be cursed by visting a Psychologist.

Status Name Effects
Schizophrenia The first stage of insanity. The player will begin to hear noises constantly
Trauma The second stage of insanity. the player will bow down and begin begging uncontrollably when another player is seen.
Maniacal 4th stage of insanity. if any player is knocked around those afflicted, they will uncontrollably try to grip the knocked player
Hallucinations The last stage of insanity. The player will begin to hallucinate a insanity monster. if the player gets too close to this monster, they will be attacked and knocked.

Temporary Status

Temporary statuses are statuses that are only caused through players. these status effects only last a couple of seconds, but are very useful in player vs player combat.

Status Name Effects
Poison Takeing damage as a percentage of health
Bleeding This state will cancle all regeneration
Black Flame When afflicted, become unable to charge mana for 5-6 seconds
Cursed When afflicted, take 30% more damage. This effect can stack for 90% more damage taken
