Lives

Rogue Lineage is a game that incorporates perma-death mechanics. perma-death mechanics refers to a gameplay loop where the players progress can be reset. In the case of Rogue Lineage, the players progress is only reset once all of their lives are gone. In the Rogue community, this is refered to as "Wiping".

Each time your character is killed, whether this is to ailments, players, or other causes, they will lose one life. Aside from Kasparan every player will spawn into the game with 3 lives.

A player can gain lives by obtaining a Pheonix Down:

Player Holding a Pheonix Down

Each pheonix down will grant the player 1 extra life. Once used, your character will be put on a 30 minute cooldown before they can use another Pheonix Down.Characters that are ultra class are allowed a maximum of 3 lives while characters at any other level of strength are allowed up to 5.

When a player loses all of their lives, they are first sent to purgatory. While here, players can meet the Ferryman who will give them 3 options:

New Character
  • This will simply restart players character. The same race is kept but name, progression, and items are all reset to 0
    • New Lineage
  • This will reroll your race 150 Robux
    • Same Character
  • This will bring your character back with a single life. costs 1000 Robux
